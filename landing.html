FAQ

Common
questions.

Do I need to create an account? +
No — just go to revaixstudy.com/app and start. No signup, no email, no credit card. The free tier gives you 3 uploads to try everything.
Does it actually know the QCE / VCE / HSC syllabus? +
Yes. In High School mode, select your state, subject and unit and the AI generates content specifically aligned to that syllabus — using the correct command verbs, assessment objectives and question styles of your state's external exams.
Do high school students need to upload anything? +
No. High school students can just select their state, subject and unit and Revaix generates a full study pack from the syllabus alone. You can optionally upload your own notes too for personalised content.
What file types can I upload? +
PDF, JPG and PNG are supported. Best results with 1–15 pages. Very large PDFs may take longer. Word and PowerPoint files need to be saved as PDF first.
How does the assignment marking work? +
Upload your completed assignment as a PDF or image. You can also upload your rubric or marking criteria for more accurate feedback. The AI reads your work and gives paragraph-by-paragraph feedback, an overall grade, and criteria scores.
Is my data private? +
Your uploaded files are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate study content and are not stored on our servers. We don't keep your documents after processing.
How much does it cost? +
Free to start with 3 uploads. The Student plan is $5/month for 20 uploads, and the Serious Student plan is $10/month for unlimited uploads. Payments aren't live yet — everything is completely free while we're in beta.
